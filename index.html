Frequently Asked Questions

How does the onboarding process work?

We start with a free discovery call to understand your business and what you need. From there, we'll get access to your accounts, do an initial review, and have you set up within a week.

What software do you work with?

We work exclusively with QuickBooks Online — the industry standard for small business bookkeeping. If you're not set up yet, we'll get you started.

How far behind can my books be?

There's no such thing as "too far behind." Whether you're a few months or a few years behind, we've seen it all and we can get you caught up without judgment.

Do you handle taxes?

We don't file taxes directly, but we prepare your books so your CPA or tax professional has everything they need. We also work alongside your accountant to make tax season seamless.

What does it cost?

Every business is different, so we customize our packages based on your needs and volume. Book a free consultation and we'll put together a plan that fits your budget.
